if the debugger is currently waiting to continue you can continue with this. Valid parameters are :co_run (just continue), co_stepinto(when on top of a call, follow it), co_stepover (when on top of a call run till after the call) return co_run,co_stepinto,co_stepover 1:0 2:1 3:2
