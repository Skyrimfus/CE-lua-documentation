Index 
repeat...until

Loop based on expression

repeat
statements
...
until <expression>



Parameters




expressionThe statements in between repeat...until are executed until the expression is true.




Remarks

The expression is tested after the loop is executed, so the loop will be executed atleast once.



Related

break, for...in...do, while...do



Example



arr = {1,2,3,4,5,6}
i = 1
repeat
 print(arr[i])
 i=i+1
until arr[i]==nil
--[[output:
1
2
3
4
5
6
]]
